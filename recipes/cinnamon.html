Cinnamon Swirl French Toast Casserole

Description

This warm, sweet cinnamon roll casserole is a great twist on French toast, kind of like a French toast casserole and custard mixed. We have it every Christmas morning. Serve with whipped cream and eiter blackberry or strawberry preserves.

by Amber

  • Prep:15 mins
  • Cook:50 mins
  • Additional:8 hrs
  • Total:9 hrs 5 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 1 casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter, or as needed
  • 1 loaf cinnamon swirl bread
  • 1 1/2 cups mil
  • 1 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Butter 6 slices of cinnamon swirl bread; place butter-side down into a casserole dish. Butter 6 more slices of bread and place butter-side down on top of other slices.
  2. Mix milk, sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, and salt together in a bowl and pour over bread. Let sit for 8 hours to overnight
  3. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  4. Place casserole dish inside a larger pan; pour water into the outside pan, making sure not to get water into the casserole.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a knifer inserted into the center comes out mostly clean, about 50 minutes
